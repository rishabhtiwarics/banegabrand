01 · Introduction

Our Commitment to Your Privacy

At BanegaBrand (operated by Rajneesh Kumar), we are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and what rights you have in relation to it when you visit our website banegabrand.com or use our consultation services.

By using our website or services, you consent to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. If you do not agree, please do not use our website or services.

02 · Information We Collect

What Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us when you:

  • Book a consultation — name, email address, phone number, referral code
  • Make a payment — transaction details processed via secure payment gateways
  • Contact us via WhatsApp, email, or our website contact forms
  • Subscribe to updates or newsletters

We also automatically collect certain information when you visit our website, including your IP address, browser type, pages visited, time spent on pages, and referring URLs. This information is collected via cookies and similar tracking technologies.

03 · How We Use Your Information

How We Use Your Data

We use the information we collect to:

  • Process your booking and confirm your consultation session
  • Provide, personalise, and improve our services
  • Send you booking confirmations, reminders, and session follow-ups
  • Respond to your queries, comments, and support requests
  • Send promotional content and updates about our services (with your consent)
  • Analyse website usage to improve user experience
  • Comply with legal obligations and prevent fraud

We will never sell your personal information to third parties. Your data is used solely to deliver and improve our services.

04 · Cookies

Cookies & Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to track activity on our website and store certain information. Cookies are small data files placed on your device.

We use the following types of cookies:

  • Essential cookies: Required for the website to function properly.
  • Analytics cookies: Help us understand how visitors interact with our website (e.g., Google Analytics).
  • Marketing cookies: Used to deliver relevant advertisements and track campaign effectiveness (e.g., Meta Pixel, Google Ads).

You can instruct your browser to refuse all cookies or indicate when a cookie is being sent. However, some parts of our website may not function properly without cookies.

05 · Data Sharing

Third-Party Sharing

We do not sell or rent your personal data. We may share your information with trusted third parties only in the following circumstances:

  • Payment processors: To securely process your payments (e.g., Razorpay, PayU). These services have their own privacy policies.
  • Analytics providers: Google Analytics and similar tools to analyse site usage in anonymised form.
  • Communication tools: WhatsApp Business, email platforms used to deliver consultation confirmations.
  • Legal requirements: If required to comply with applicable law, court orders, or government requests.

All third parties are contractually required to handle your data securely and in accordance with applicable data protection laws.

06 · Data Retention

How Long We Keep Your Data

We retain your personal data only for as long as necessary to fulfil the purposes outlined in this policy, or as required by applicable law. Specifically:

  • Booking and consultation records are retained for a minimum of 3 years for business and tax compliance purposes.
  • Marketing preferences and communications data are retained until you unsubscribe.
  • Website analytics data is retained in anonymised form.

Once data is no longer required, it is securely deleted or anonymised.

07 · Your Rights

Your Rights Over Your Data

Under applicable Indian data protection laws, you have the following rights:

  • Access: You may request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correction: You may ask us to correct inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Deletion: You may request that we delete your personal data, subject to legal retention obligations.
  • Objection: You may object to processing of your data for marketing purposes at any time.
  • Withdrawal of Consent: Where processing is based on consent, you may withdraw it at any time by contacting us.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at support@banegabrand.com. We will respond within 30 days.

08 · Security

Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organisational security measures to protect your personal information against unauthorised access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. These include encrypted connections (HTTPS), secure payment gateways, and restricted access to personal data within our team.

While we strive to protect your information, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security but commit to notifying you in the event of a data breach that affects your personal information.

09 · Children

Children's Privacy

Our services are intended for individuals aged 18 years and above. We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately and we will delete such data.

10 · External Links

Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to third-party websites including YouTube, Instagram, Facebook, and WhatsApp. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these external sites. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of any third-party sites you visit.

11 · Updates

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. If changes are significant, we will notify you via email or a prominent notice on our website. Continued use of our services after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the revised policy.

Questions About Your Privacy?

If you have any questions, concerns, or wish to exercise your data rights, please contact us. We are committed to addressing your privacy concerns promptly.
